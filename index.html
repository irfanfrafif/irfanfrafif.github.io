About Me

I’m Irfan Fadilah Rafif, a Computer Science student at Universitas Gadjah Mada with a strong passion for game development. I enjoy creating immersive and engaging gameplay mechanics. My experience spans working with tools like Unity, developing custom systems for games, and crafting unique game ideas for game jams.

I’ve had the honor of winning awards for some of my games, which are the main highlights of my portfolio. Beyond that, I’ve worked on smaller projects that showcase my skills in programming, design, and problem-solving.

What I love about game development:

Designing creative gameplay systems.
Exploring the technical side, from physics to shader.
Blending art, technology, and storytelling into unique experiences.

I use Unity as my primary game engine, leveraging its versatility to create engaging and dynamic projects. While C# is my go-to programming language, I also know Java to a certain extent, which helps me adapt to various development needs. For game development purposes, I am quite familiar with Blender and Photoshop, which I use for creating and refining 3D models, textures, and other visual assets to enhance the overall game design.

In addition to games, I also explores a bit about AI and web, combining my technical background with creativity to bring ideas to life.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and see the projects I’ve worked on. If you'd want, don’t hesitate to reach out!
